About the company CHTUP "Agrovitaservice"

CHTUP "Agrovitaservice" is a company offering the latest technological solutions for successful dairy farming in the Republic of Belarus and the CIS countries. For our clients, we offer only the best, proven and proven technologies and equipment from Western Europe and America.

Among our products are milking machines, milking parlors, equipment and equipment for farms, nipple rubber, livestock mats and much more in the product catalog.

Our team of specialists has accumulated extensive experience in the areas of milking, cooling and storage of milk, ventilation, watering, comfort of keeping cattle, technologies for cleaning, transportation, homogenization, separation and storage of manure. We are confident that only innovation will allow agricultural enterprises to obtain milk of only the highest quality and achieve the most effective economic indicators.

We offer turnkey construction of dairy complexes, modernization and re-equipment of existing dairy farms. We provide full technical and consulting support for the optimal design of MTC for the Customer’s needs, we are actively engaged in the supply of spare parts, installation, commissioning and service work.
